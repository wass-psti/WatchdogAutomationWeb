Engineering

Engineering

Build real automation systems that power industries.

As part of the Watchdog engineering team, you'll work hands-on with PLC programming, SCADA integration, instrumentation, and control panel design across a wide range of industrial projects — locally and internationally.

Apply Now

Requirements

What we're looking for

PLC Programming

Experience with Siemens TIA Portal (S7-300/1200/1500) or equivalent platforms.

HMI / SCADA

Familiarity with WinCC, FactoryTalk, or similar SCADA/HMI environments.

Instrumentation

Knowledge of pressure, temperature, flow, and level transmitters.

Problem Solver

Ability to troubleshoot control systems under project pressure.

Team Player

Works well with project engineers, clients, and field technicians.

Educational Background

BS in Electrical, Electronics, Instrumentation, or Mechatronics Engineering preferred.

Responsibilities

What you'll be doing

Program and commission PLC and SCADA systems for industrial clients.

Conduct Factory Acceptance Testing (FAT) and Site Acceptance Testing (SAT).

Design and review electrical schematics and control panel layouts.

Perform loop checks and instrument calibration on-site.

Collaborate with project engineers to deliver solutions on time.

Prepare technical documentation and turnover packages.
