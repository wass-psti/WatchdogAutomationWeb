Internship / OJT

Internship / On-the-Job Training

Start your engineering career with real-world experience.

Our OJT program gives students hands-on exposure to industrial automation — from PLC programming to site commissioning. You won't be filing papers; you'll be working alongside our engineers on actual projects.

Apply Now

Requirements

What we're looking for

Engineering Student

Currently enrolled in BS Electrical, Electronics, Instrumentation, or Mechatronics Engineering.

Eager to Learn

Willingness to learn in a fast-paced environment and take on real responsibilities.

Team-Oriented

Comfortable working alongside engineers, technicians, and project managers.

Responsibilities

What you'll be doing

Assist engineers in PLC and SCADA programming tasks.

Support on-site commissioning and loop checking activities.

Help prepare technical documentation and project reports.

Learn hands-on wiring and control panel assembly techniques.

Observe and participate in Factory Acceptance Testing (FAT).

Shadow senior engineers across different project phases.

Ready to apply for OJT?

Send your resume, endorsement letter from your school, and a brief introduction. Let us know your preferred start date and required number of hours.
